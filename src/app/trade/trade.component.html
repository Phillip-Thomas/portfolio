Algorithmic Trading


I have spent the larger portion of my life heavily interested in trading/investing. Despite this vast interest however, as one can imagine, I mostly just lost money when starting out at the age of 18. I then continued to lose money, albeit to a lesser extent, for nearly a decade before I finally understood how to be consistently profitable.

While attempting to explain the recipe for profitability is a bit beyond the scope of this portfolio, I will say that I eventually found the primary problem to be that I am not a machine and thus am subject to vulnerable behaviours such as emotion, greed, and sleep. To remidy this, I dedicated much of my time to translating my trading logic into code and building the architecture necessary to implement it. Now, I just sit back and watch as my logic is implemented on dozens of cryptocurrencies simultanesouly on time frames most humans just can't keep up with.

To implement these strategies, I've written several scripts with various purpose in a language known as Pine Script, A programming language developed by Trading View. These strategies provide signals on charts provided by Trading View which can be based on any number of indicators. These signals when fired send a webhook to a REST api I've built. Here, I use node.js to perform any logic not directly related to signaling such as money management and risk/reward analysis. A new webhook is then fired to an exchange which places the orders and relays the information back for accounting.

Over the years, these strategies have grown and grown in complexity. I now consistently beat the market, mitigate the drawdown of buying and holding, and all while I'm off living my life! Very soon these strategies will be available for subscription usage!
